Weihao XUAN (宣 偉豪)

I am a Ph.D. student at Machine Learning and Statistical Data Analysis Lab (杉山・横矢・石田研究室), The University of Tokyo (東京大学), where I am very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Naoto Yokoya. I am also under the Junior Research Associate (JRA) program at RIKEN Center for Advanced Intelligence Project.

I obtained a master's degree from Waseda University. Prior to that, I received my bachelor's degree with First-Class Honours in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Leeds, United Kingdom.

I also work very closely with Dr. Aoran Xiao (Nanyang Technological University, Singapore) and Heli Qi (National Institute of Informatics, Japan).

I am open to potential collaborations and discussions. Please do not hesitate to contact me should you have any proposals or questions.
