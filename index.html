Research and Selected Publications

My current research focuses on employing limited labeled data for deep learning in the context of large-scale scene understanding. Specifically, my focusing areas include:

  • Multi-Modal & Cross-Modal Learning (Vision&Language, Image&Point Clouds, RGB-D...)

  • Point Cloud Analysis (3D Semantic Segmentation, 3D Object Detection...)
