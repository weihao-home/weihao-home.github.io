Weihao XUAN (宣 偉豪)

I'm a Ph.D. candidate at Machine Learning and Statistical Data Analysis Lab (杉山・横矢・石田研究室), The University of Tokyo (東京大学), where I'm very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Naoto Yokoya. I'm also under the Junior Research Associate (JRA) program at RIKEN Center for Advanced Intelligence Project.

My main research focuses on natural language understanding, particularly in evaluation and post-training. I'm also actively engaged in AI for Social Good, applying NLP techniques and foundation models to Earth Observation and Medical domains through collaborative research. I'm fortunate to work with amazing collaborators worldwide, including close friends and brilliant researchers.
